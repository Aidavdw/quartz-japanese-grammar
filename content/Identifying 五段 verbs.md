having a verb that ends in る, it can be either an [[一段]] or a [[五段]] verb.
Identifying them is easy
Godan: /a/・/u/・/o/ る  
作る、転がる、折る
Ichidan: /e/・/i/ る - **With exceptions!**
食べる、浴びる
