a [[Particle]]
# Transitional space
for example, denotes location to leave
>バズ==を==降りる
>Getting **off** the bus.

Also 'going through'
>公園==を==走る
>Running **through** the park.

# Taking an action in a direction
>左==を==見る
>Looking towards the left (turning your head)

>左を曲がる
>to turn (a vehicle) to the left direction.
