from [[こそあど (ここ、そこ, あそこ, etc)]] どう, but nominalised with [[か]].
# Whether or not
Used with [[か]] どうか. 
>==先生が学校に行ったかどうか==知らない
>I don't know **whether or not** teacher went to school

>==行くかどうか==、確認しています
>I will confirm **whether or not** we will go.

Often seen with [[Question word]], such as 何かどうか
