{止|や}む= Lit. to stop

Can be used as an auxiliary verb to denote you stop doing a thing. Doesn't imply whether you finish, just that you quit doing it.
>子供がやっと==泣きやんだ==
>The child finally stopped crying.

>目覚まし時計が==鳴:り止みません==
>The alarm clock won't stop ringing
